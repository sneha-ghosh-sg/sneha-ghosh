Pricing & Monetisation

Pricing a net-new AI product from zero — Freddy AI Agent

Challenge

Freshworks was launching an AI Agent product into a crowded market (Agentforce, Intercom, HubSpot) with no established pricing precedent for agentic AI and no internal playbook.

Approach
  • Evaluated session-based, consumption, and outcome-based pricing models; defined SKU constructs and entitlement logic
  • Benchmarked Tier-1 competitors on pricing structure, packaging tiers, and discount posture to stress-test positioning
  • Built a 3-year financial model with adoption curves, cost-to-serve assumptions, and sensitivity analysis across volume scenarios
  • Designed an AI Agent ROI Calculator modelling customer-specific value across deflection, productivity, and cost savings — iterated on field feedback and drove global GTM rollout
  • Authored discount guardrails, tier-eligibility criteria, and pricing FAQs; handled escalations for non-standard deal structures
Result

Investment case approved by executive leadership. Positioned for ~$10M ARR with 4x growth in session volumes. 2x projected revenue achieved within two quarters. ROI Calculator adopted as a standard asset in CXO-level enterprise deal cycles globally.

Product-Led Growth

Redesigning onboarding to reduce drop-off — Freshworks CRM & Freshsales

Challenge

New signups on two CRM products weren't reaching activation milestones. Drop-off was concentrated in the first few sessions — before users had explored enough of the product to convert or expand.

Approach
  • Mapped drop-off points using Heap behavioral data and Appcues session analytics across both products
  • Redesigned onboarding modal sequences to surface core features at the right moment in the user journey
  • Introduced contextual nudges and quick-help tooltips to guide users without interrupting workflow
  • Built feature-based user profiles and progressive badges to incentivize deeper product exploration
  • Coordinated shipping with engineering, content, and design; iterated based on in-product funnel data
Result

Modal CTR up 11% on FW CRM and 8% on Freshsales. Feature utilization increased 15%. Packaging alignment experiments run in parallel drove a 20% uplift in top-of-funnel volumes and a $3M ARR contribution from chatbot monetisation.

GTM Strategy & Operations

Challenge

FW CRM was designated a focus product in India with no existing market playbook, no dedicated team, and no pipeline. I owned strategy, operations, marketing, and BD for the programme.

Approach

  • Built persona-driven value propositions, battlecards, and executive-grade decks for mid-market buyers
  • Owned pipeline generation end-to-end across partner interlocks, product-led sales plays, and integrated marketing campaigns
Result

$1.2M ARR pipeline in 4 months. Programme adopted as the template for subsequent focus-product GTM motions at Freshworks. Recognised with the Excellence Award for GTM Strategic Programs (FY'23).
