Pricing & Monetisation

Pricing a net-new AI product from zero — Freddy AI Agent

Challenge

Freshworks was launching an AI Agent product into a crowded market (competing with Agentforce, Intercom, HubSpot) with no established pricing precedent for agentic AI and no internal playbook to draw from.

Approach

I owned the full lifecycle — evaluated session-based, consumption, and outcome-based models; defined SKU constructs and entitlement logic; benchmarked Tier-1 competitors on pricing structure, packaging tiers, and discount posture; built a 3-year financial model with adoption curves and cost-to-serve sensitivity analysis; developed an interactive ROI Calculator that models customer-specific value across deflection, productivity, and cost savings; authored all GTM collaterals and led company-wide enablement across Sales, CS, and partners globally.

Result

Investment case approved by executive leadership. Freddy AI Agent positioned for ~$10M ARR with 4x growth in session volumes. 2x projected revenue achieved within two quarters. ROI Calculator adopted as a standard asset in CXO-level enterprise deal cycles globally.

Revenue Operations & Monetisation Policy

Closing a $13M monetisation gap through policy redesign

Challenge

Freshworks had two silent revenue leaks: chatbot usage that had been offered for free was not converting to paid, and high discounting across top products was eroding ARR without a governance mechanism.

Approach

I identified the gaps through policy analysis and cross-functional data reviews. I redesigned chatbot session entitlements to convert previously free usage into a monetised tier with clear segment-level thresholds. Separately, I designed and implemented programmatic escalator clauses across the product portfolio — creating automatic revenue recovery mechanisms tied to usage growth — and drove execution in partnership with RevOps and Finance.

Result

~$13M ARR contribution in FY'25. Both initiatives are now embedded in standard commercial and renewal processes. Recognised with the Gold Award for Above & Beyond Performance in Monetisation Initiatives (FY'25).

GTM Strategy & Operations

Building a $1.2M pipeline in 4 months — India CRM market entry

Challenge

FW CRM was designated a focus product in India with no existing market playbook, no dedicated team, and no pipeline. I was the sole owner of strategy, operations, marketing, and BD — effectively a one-person founder for this GTM motion.

Approach

Mapped the Indian CRM market, defined TAM across high-growth verticals (FinTech, EdTech, Travel, Healthcare), prioritised segments by technology adoption readiness and competitive whitespace, and built a revenue forecast model. Recruited and program-managed a cross-functional team of 14 across Product, Sales, and PMM. Designed industry-specific sales playbooks that shifted messaging from features to business outcomes; built persona-driven value propositions, battlecards, and executive-grade decks for mid-market buyers. Owned pipeline generation end-to-end across partner interlocks, product-led sales plays, and integrated marketing campaigns.

Result

$1.2M ARR pipeline in 4 months. Programme served as the template for subsequent focus-product GTM motions at Freshworks. Recognised with the Excellence Award for GTM Strategic Programs (FY'23).
